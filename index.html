Vertical Farming

Vertical Farming is a modern development that utilizes existing acreage, or even reduced acreage in order to grow and produce more crops. By using human-made and human-controlled greenhouses and environments, crops are able to grow and mature year-round while using less water thanks to less pollution from chemicals and effective software regulation systems. Our design is to take the development of Vertical Farming, and push it into a industrial production wherever needed.

Due to the ability to grow within urban areas, we would be able to quickly ship and deliver freshly grown produce to nearby markets and grocery stores at a reduced shipping rate. In addition, people would have the choice to instead purchase produce directly from us, the farmers, without going through a secondary outlet. Thanks to this opportunity to be able to directly communicate and collaborate with communities with crops and produce, expansion and adoption of vertical farming would grow rapidly, and we would be right there to shape that growth.
